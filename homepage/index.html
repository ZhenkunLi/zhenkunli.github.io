Summary

Zhenkun Li is a Ph.D. researcher at Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering, University of Alberta. Prior to joining UA, he received his Master's degree in School of Civil Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China. His research interests lie in the Structural Health Monitoring(SHM) for civil infrastructure systems, damage detection, finite element analysis, machine learning and computer vision.
