About me

Short summary

If you are interested in structural health monitoring (SHM), vehicle-bridge interaction (VBI), and machine learning, and intend to cooperate with me, please contact me at zhenkun.li@aalto.fi.

Zhenkun Li is a Researcher at the Department of Civil Engineering at Aalto University. He received his M.Eng. in School of Civil Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China. His research interests lie in the Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) for civil infrastructure systems, damage detection, finite element analysis, deep learning and computer vision.
