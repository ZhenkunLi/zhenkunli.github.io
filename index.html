Summary

Currently, Zhenkun Li is looking for a Ph.D. position related to Structural Health Monitoring(SHM). He received his M.Sc. in School of Civil Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China. His research interests lie in the SHM for civil infrastructure systems, damage detection, finite element analysis, machine learning and computer vision. Before this, he worked as a structural algorithm engineer at Beijing Smartbow Information Technology Co. Ltd where he was responsible for developing Operational Modal Analysis (OMA) tools. (CV)
