Summary

Currently, Zhenkun Li is a doctoral candidate at Aalto University in Finland. He received his M.Eng. in School of Civil Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China. His research interests lie in the Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) for civil infrastructure systems, damage detection, finite element analysis, deep learning and computer vision.
