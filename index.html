Research Interests

Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)

Damage detection, Model updating, modal identification.

Vehicle-bridge Interaction (VBI)

Vehicle scanning method, Weigh-in-motion (WIM), Vehicular parameter identification, Road roughness estimation.

Machine Learning

Bayesian statistics, Gaussian processes, Deep learning, Computer vision, Transfer learning, Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs).

Research purposes

Our infrastructures, especially bridges, were built in the last century and are prone to deterioration and exposed to environmental hazards. My research aims to monitor the health conditions of infrastructures via system identification, uncertainty quantification, interaction between bridges and vehicles, and the cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques. If you are interested in my background and intend to cooperate with me, please contact me at zhenkun.li@aalto.fi.
